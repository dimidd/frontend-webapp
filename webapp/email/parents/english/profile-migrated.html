We are writing to let you know that BabysitterJLM (a website for connecting parents & babysitters in Jerusalem, that you previously signed up to) is becoming Popinz - a new and improved website.

We have moved your profile to our new website, to set your account password click this link - https://popinz.co/index.html#/reset/en

What’s new on Popinz?

  • More babysitters (760 and counting)
  • Better babysitter profiles give you more information about the babysitter including detailed experience and availability.
  • You’re in control: search for babysitters in your neighborhood and reach out to the ones you like best.
  • Better user experience: everything is automated, fast and clear

What happens now?

Log in to your profile with the above link which will expire in 30 days. Update your profile if you’d like and search for babysitters that suit your needs.
Your account is private and your information will only be shared with babysitters if and when you contact them.

What you can do with Popinz:

  • Find babysitters in your neighborhood that are available when you need them! Filter by language or location, and learn more through our detailed profiles.
  • Contact Babysitters - Reach out to relevant babysitters and coordinate.
  • Have a Blast - Go on that date you deserve, try that new class, or do anything else!

Feel free to share with family and friends (they’ll thank you) and send great babysitters our way. Follow our blog ,Facebook and Pinterest pages for fun parenting tips, ideas and activities!
